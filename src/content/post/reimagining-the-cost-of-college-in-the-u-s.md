Our own Director of Savings Programs, Beth Williams, wrote a piece for [IDEO](https://challenges.openideo.com/challenge/higher-ed/research/universal-children-s-savings-accounts-at-birth) on how we might reimagine the cost of college in the U.S. and how it’s paid for.

“An effective solution exists – and it does not require new financial resources, rather just a reallocation of dollars already committed to higher education. We need to make a commitment to our nation’s young people by investing in their futures earlier – at birth.”
