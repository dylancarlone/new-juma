When our founders opened the doors of their first social enterprise, a single Ben and Jerry’s Scoop Shop, they had one goal: to give homeless youth a bridge back to opportunity. Since then, Juma has grown and expanded across the nation and served over 7,200 young people who have earned over $9.5 million in pursuit of that goal. Hear some of our earliest supporters discuss why they got behind this bold vision 25 years ago and why they continue to believe in the importance of Juma’s mission today.
