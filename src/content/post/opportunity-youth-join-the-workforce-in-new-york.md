Last year, Juma launched our social enterprise operation at Yankee’s Stadium in New York and this year at Turner Field in Atlanta. This season, we are excited to be employing 140 opportunity youth at Yankee Stadium and 100 at Turner Field. We are proud to be serving opportunity youth who represent 5.6 million young people in this country who are out-of-work and out-of-school.

Juma’s recent recruitment efforts in Atlanta and NYC were well-received with hundreds of youth showing up to be interviewed. We are thankful to our partners, New York Children’s Aid Society, BronxWorks, My Brother’s Keeper, and the Fulton County who helped with recruitment efforts. We’d also like to thank Regions Bank in Atlanta and Neighborhood Trust Financial Partners in NYC for their partnership which provides financial capability training to Juma youth in each of these cities.
