As we get deeper into the year and look forward to new challenges and opportunities, we want to take a moment to thank you and remind you of what your support has meant to Juma youth. Through the past two years of unprecedented hardship, supporters like you have been a lifeline to Juma, allowing us to be there, to show up for the young people who need it most. Thank you for your continued support and for being a part of our community. #StrongerTogether
