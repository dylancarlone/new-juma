We are excited to welcome Steve Wolfe, Ahsha (Merson) Haggart and Willie Alford II, to Juma’s National Board of Directors!  

**Steve Wolfe** is the co-founder of Growth Street Partners, a San Francisco-based growth equity firm. He brings to Juma a wealth of business and financial expertise, which he will put to good use as he leads the Board’s Finance Committee.

**Ahsha (Merson) Haggart** is a Principal of TPG Sixth Street Partners, based in San Francisco, and focused on strategy and capital formation. She will be joining the Board’s Development Committee to which she brings a strong background in finance and investment as well as prior experience serving on event and development committees.  

**Willie Alford II**, Managing Director, Blackrock. Willie leads the Foundation and Endowment effort in the Western U.S., which provides investment management services to nonprofit institutions. Juma will benefit from Willie’s deep knowledge of the sector as well as his expertise in business development.
