“Juma helped me through my studies in high school. They’ve helped me get my college applications done. Now that I’m in college, I’m still getting support for all kinds of things that are still coming my way as an 18-year-old freshman in college.”

\-Kory Eastland

Check out this [CBS story](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w9LqhvzR32s) and the [50 Fund’s Game Changer video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gfNY8cejDEM) to see how the Super Bowl 50 Fund provided Juma with support to deepen and grow our Bay Area work, creating more opportunities for youth like Kory and Erick to work, graduate from high school, and save money for college. We are thrilled to report that over 120 Juma youth worked at Levi’s Stadium for SB50 and earned a game-changing $14K that will help them with college expenses!
