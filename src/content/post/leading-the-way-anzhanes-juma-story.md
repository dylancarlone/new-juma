On October 28th, at Juma’s 30th Anniversary Paths to Possibility celebration, Juma Alumna Anzhane Slaughter shared how for her, it all started with the Juma job. For Anzhane, Juma was a first chance to recognize her talent as a leader and practice those skills in the real world. Today, she’s the founder and CEO of her own business, Young Black Homeowners, a successful real-estate agent, an entrepreneur, and a community leader. Learn more about how Anzhane used Juma as a springboard to uplift herself and her community in the video above.
