We are grateful to report that we’ve been in contact with our Juma family in Houston and Dallas since the terrible developments of Hurricane Harvey and all of our Juma Youth and colleagues and their families are safe. We will continue to stay in close communication as the storm continues to ravage the Gulf Coast.

Our thoughts and prayers are with Houston and everyone affected by Harvey.
