Dear Juma Community,

On behalf of the Juma Ventures Board of Directors, I am pleased to announce the appointment of Dr. Lisa Chin as Juma’s next Chief Executive Officer, effective January 1, 2026.

Lisa brings to Juma more than two decades of executive leadership in youth-serving organizations, including her tenure as CEO of Treehouse, which supports the educational success of foster youth, as President & CEO of the Boys & Girls Club of King County, and as Founding Executive Director of YearUp in Seattle. Throughout her career, Lisa has demonstrated a steadfast commitment to youth empowerment, a track record of strategic growth, and a deep belief in the transformative power of opportunity.

Lisa shared, “I am honored to join an organization that has been doing such incredible work for young people for more than 30 years. I look forward to partnering with our Board, staff, donors, and community supporters to build on Juma’s successes and expand opportunities, ensuring every young person we serve has the tools and support they need to thrive.”

The Juma Board of Directors is confident that Lisa’s vision, experience, and energy will guide Juma into a powerful next chapter—one grounded in impact, growth, and an unwavering focus on driving lifelong outcomes for the young people we serve.

I would also like to express the Board’s profound gratitude to Abdiel Cerrud, who has served as Interim CEO during this transition. Abdiel’s leadership, focus, and deep dedication to Juma’s mission have ensured continuity and strength throughout this period.

As we look ahead to 2026, Juma remains focused on expanding economic opportunity for young people through employment, financial empowerment coaching, and connection to pathways that lead to opportunity. With Lisa at the helm, and with your continued support for our mission, we are poised to deepen our impact at a time when our work is more critical than ever.

We are grateful to all of you, our community of supporters. Your unwavering commitment enables us to navigate this leadership transition with stability and focus on the future.

Please join me and the entire Board of Directors in welcoming Lisa to the Juma family and in celebrating Abdiel for his remarkable interim leadership in these challenging times.

With gratitude,
