Juma Ventures respects the privacy of our donors and provides this privacy policy to inform donors as to how personal information is used and opportunities for opting out of some Juma practices.

Juma collects and uses donor information, such as name, address, phone number, and email address, when a donor voluntarily provides it to us. Juma Ventures keeps a record of each donor's giving history and uses this information to understand donor needs and interests and to provide donors with better service. Use of donor information is limited to the above and to the internal purposes of Juma Ventures for furthering the stated mission and programs of the agency.

Specifically, we use donor information to help donors complete a secure donation transaction and to provide communication and updates on Juma's youth development programs. In addition to utilizing SSL encryption for all transactions, Juma stores information on isolated network servers, kept in a physically secure environment with strict policies that minimize staff access to the stored information. Credit card numbers are used only for donation or payment processing and are not retained by Juma.

Juma Ventures will not trade, share, sell, rent or lease your personal information with anyone else, nor will Juma Ventures send you mailings on behalf of other organizations. Routinely, donor names are published in lists to recognize donor support. However, we respect anonymity, should a supporter prefer not to be listed as a Juma donor. We provide donors with the opportunity to remove their name from our mailing list if desired, as it is not our wish to send unwanted mail to our donors. Please contact us if you wish to be fully or partially removed from our mailing list.

Juma views donors as valued community partners, and we take seriously each recommendation as to how we might improve communication. Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please call our Development Department at 415.371.0727, ext. 207.
