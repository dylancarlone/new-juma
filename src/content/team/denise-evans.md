Denise Evans a nonprofit finance leader with more than a decade of experience building financial systems and organizational infrastructure that help mission-driven organizations thrive. As Director of Finance at Juma Ventures, Denise provides strategic leadership for financial planning, accounting, compliance, and operational decision-making in support of the organization’s workforce development mission. Prior to joining Juma Ventures, Denise served as Director of Accounting and Finance at Friends of Waterfront Park, where they led financial operations for the nonprofit steward of Seattle’s new 20-acre Waterfront Park. During their tenure, Denise strengthened financial systems, guided budgeting and forecasting, supported executive leadership and board decision-making, and helped position the organization for long-term sustainability as it transitioned from capital campaign completion to ongoing operations. Earlier in their career, Denise held finance and accounting roles across the nonprofit and private sectors, including positions with the National Asian Pacific Center on Aging, Raymond James & Associates, and Meeting Street. They hold a Bachelor of Science in Accounting from Johnson & Wales University.
